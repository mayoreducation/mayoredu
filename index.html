About MAYOR

Mayor Education Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. is an educational consultancy devoted towards providing abroad education and visa related services to the Nepalese. We assist students enabling them to make proper decisions regarding their further studies. Our knowledge in this field has made us a time-tested resource in helping students to gain admission for suitable countries in reputed institutions across the World. At MEC, we firmly believe that further education in abroad country requires proper planning to ensure that students choose the suitable academic pathway. With this in mind, we assist students by giving comprehensive information about study opportunities abroad. We believe in providing professional guidance and services to students, parents and institutions we represent at all times. Further, MEC having an organized administrative setup and in-depth knowledge of the market norms, we offer the best visa services to our clients for all countries. People who wish to travel to foreign countries can avail our services that are offered with the said time frame. Applying for visa incurs several procedures. Our professionals offer these services in the most convenient way.
