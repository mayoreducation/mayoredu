VISION

We seek to explore the scope and future opportunity of different subjects available around the world and present it to the people of Nepal for their prospective and potential future.

Our vision is to create a structured, secure and nurturing atmosphere where we can provide informed guidance to students, so that they can make proper and wiser decisions regarding their future studies.

Apart from this, at MEC, our team really makes an environment to guide people who seek to travel abroad for various other reasons. Our expertise knowledge in visa services makes our company a one-stop solution for all those who wish to travel to other countries for studies or various other purposes.

At MEC, clients receive an environment that is conducive to learning, inspiring, growing, and encouraging each individual to reach their full potential while learning about the diverse transitions of Secure Abroad Future life to ensure that they are ready for the transition regarding culture, language, and educational processes of their chosen learning destinations.

So, our core vision is to inspire a wave of customers who have that will-power and determination, by assisting them to acquire the required knowledge and exposure to contribute towards the development of the nation.

MISSION

To be focused on providing guidance-related services that are dedicated to ensuring a stable and consistent experience for the well-being of our customers.

To establish a family-like atmosphere of growth and development within our team and clients; wherein, each team member will have a responsibility in providing a positive, well-rounded environment to the clients.

To analyze each individual's processing on a personal level and invest in developing their perspective towards successful visa applications for any purpose. Through effective proper, effective, and precise counseling, we promote responsible and justified procedures in our clients for a convenient and hassle-free experience.

To ease the complication by providing our support and guidance during the process of getting a temporarily travel visa. Without a visa, entering another territory will be illegal and any country's law does not tolerate such acts. We help our customers avoid all these kinds of problems in the best possible way.

OBJECTIVE

We possess a specialized department of visa offering proficient and value-enhanced services to help you with all your visa-related queries, ensuring that you have a truly enriching travel experience.

Provide students with proper guidance according to their Personal, Academic & Financial needs and requirements.

We provide pre-departure orientation to give an idea as there is a visa validity imposed on each and every individual, which implies that for any person there is a limited amount of time to stay within or to leave a country. Our orientation programs help customers keep on track with their travel to avoid legal issues.

Make sure that each and every procedure is strictly followed in the visa processes and clients are served at ease to freely travel to their desired destination.

Promote the abroad-related study opportunities and their faculties in Nepal with the help of esteemed and renowned educational institutes from Canada, Australia, UK, USA.

GOALS

Our goal is to understand the requirements of individuals and assist them in the proper selection of documents and guide them with various other requirements to be fulfilled. There are different types of visas, for example, student visa, transit visa, tourist visa, business visa, temporary working visa, etc. each visa takes a certain amount of validity time. So, we make sure that individuals are provided with a visa that aligns with their interest the best.

Also, we ensure that they are well informed about the restrictions and legal issues they might face while being in a foreign land and the necessary precautions to take in mind before traveling to their destinations.
